
A4 = Alt[4]
https://groupprops.subwiki.org/wiki/Subgroup_structure_of_alternating_group:A4
https://groupprops.subwiki.org/wiki/Alternating_group:A4


A4 is the group of smallest possible order (in this case 12) not having subgroups of all orders dividing the group order.

[A4 is non-abelian]
    [n>=4] ==>> [Alt[n] is non-abelian]
[center(A4) is trivial] # centerless
[A4 has no cyclic normal subgroup]


Automorphism class of subgroups of A4:
    #no subgroups of order 6
    * trivial
        order=1
    * C[2]
        order=2
    * V4=C[2]^2
        order=4
    * A3=C[3]
        order=3
    * A4 <- V4 ><| C[3]
        order=12
Automorphism class of normal subgroups of A4:
    * trivial
    * V4
    * A4
