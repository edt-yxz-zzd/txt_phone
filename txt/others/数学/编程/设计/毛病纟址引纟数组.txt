py 使用 负址引:
  [ls[-1] is ls[len(ls)-1]]
  [ls[:-1] === ls[:len(ls)-1]]
  [ls[:] === ls[:len(ls)]]

但若取出特定长度的切片:
  [ls[:-len(tail)] =!= ls[:len(ls)-len(tail)]]
      毛病出在:当[len(tail)==0]

应当这样:
  『-1』代表『讫址纟数组』/end
  [ls[-2] is ls[len(ls)-1]]
  [ls[:-2] === ls[:len(ls)-1]]
  [ls[:-1] === ls[:len(ls)]]
  [ls[:-1-len(tail)] === ls[:len(ls)-len(tail)]]
